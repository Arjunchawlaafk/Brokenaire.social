Privacy Policy




At Brokenaire.social, your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy outlines the types of personal information that we collect, how it is used, and how we protect it. By using our website, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect two types of information from users: personal data and usage data.

1. Personal Data

We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or contact details unless voluntarily provided by the user (e.g., via email inquiries). Please refrain from submitting confidential or sensitive personal data on our platform.

2. Usage Data

When you visit our website, we automatically collect certain information about how you interact with our site. This includes information such as your IP address, browser type, the pages you visit, and the time and date of your visit. This data is collected to improve the user experience on the site.

Use of Data

News Data: We display specific news articles sourced from the NewsData API. These articles are provided as is, with no alterations. No personal data is shared with the NewsData API.

Cookies

Our website may collect minimal cookies for basic functionality, though we do not use them for tracking purposes or to collect personal information. While we do rely on external APIs to provide news content, these services do not collect personal data from our users. We do not store cookies on your device beyond what is necessary for the site to function smoothly.

Data Security

We are committed to protecting your personal data. While no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, we implement reasonable measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

External Links

Our website may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us. We have no control over the content and practices of these external sites and are not responsible for their privacy policies.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. We will notify users of any significant changes by posting the updated policy on this page with the effective date.
