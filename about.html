About us

Who are we ?




Welcome to Brokenaire, the online sanctuary where we embrace the chaos of life’s financial rollercoaster. Ever wondered what happens when stocks crash harder than your Wi-Fi signal? Or when a scam is so smooth it makes a used car salesman look like a saint? We’ve got you covered!

Brokenaire is your go-to source for all things not-so-glamorous in the world of finance. From epic market crashes to the kind of scams that make you wonder if you missed a memo on the latest pyramid scheme, we cover it all. If your portfolio looks like it’s taken a trip to the Bermuda Triangle, you’ll find solace in knowing you're not alone!

Grab a seat, grab a coffee (you'll need it), and dive into our world of financial fiascos. No financial crisis too big, no business loss too small – we’ve got the stories that’ll make you feel better about your own investments. Or at least make you laugh while you cry over them.
